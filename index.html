Welcome to the home page of Brooks family history.

This page and it's child pages are written and maintained by myself, Andy Brooks. When my father passed away early 2019 I received his collection of family history documents. From there I have been able to supplement that collection with many other findings across the web and from reaching out to some extremely helpful librarians. In the pages beyond, the reader will find a variety of family information along various family lines.

  1. Letters from Charles Brooks during WWII
  2. Family genealogy documents
  3. Montgomery history and society documents
  4. Sexton family documents
  5. Family veterans
